

## Optimize SEO Keywords for HNW Investment Migration Niche

### Problem
Current titles/descriptions use generic terms ("CBI", "second passport", "EU residency") that compete with low-intent informational searches. The niche is **ultra-high-net-worth investment migration** — buyers search with specific intent: program names, jurisdictions, attorney/family-office terminology, dollar thresholds, tax/mobility benefits. We need to layer in high-conversion niche keywords.

### Niche keyword research (HNW investment migration)

**Tier 1 — Commercial intent (HNW-specific)**
- "investment migration" / "investor visa" (vs generic "immigration")
- "second citizenship" (vs "second passport" — higher intent, used by HNW)
- "residency by investment" / "RBI" (industry-standard alongside CBI)
- "global mobility" / "Plan B citizenship"
- "wealth protection" / "tax residency"
- "family office" / "private client"

**Tier 2 — Program-specific long-tail**
- "[Country] passport program" / "[Country] investor program"
- "Caribbean second citizenship"
- "EU passport by investment" / "EU residency by investment"
- "E-2 visa treaty country" (Grenada — huge US-buyer intent)
- "fast-track citizenship" (São Tomé, Sierra Leone, Dominica)
- "Schengen residency" (all GV programs)

**Tier 3 — Buyer-persona modifiers**
- "for US citizens" / "for Americans" / "for HNW families"
- "2026" (current year — captures trend searches)
- "advisory" / "consultancy" (vs "agency" — HNW prefer)
